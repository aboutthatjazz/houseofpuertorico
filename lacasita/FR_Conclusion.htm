Conclusion

The House of Puerto Rico San Diego is in need of funding to carry out a joint project with the City of San Diego to build a cultural and historic cottage in Balboa Park. Once the cottage is built, it will become a property of the City of San Diego. The House of Puerto Rico San Diego will be responsible for carrying out all the cultural and educational programs at the cottage. It will also be responsible for all expenses incurred in delivering the programs.
