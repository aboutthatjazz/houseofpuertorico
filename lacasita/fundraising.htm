La Casita Cottage Funding Efforts

The House of Puerto Rico San Diego (HPRSD) is one of the national groups that is seeking to build its own cottage. Adding a cottage for the House of Puerto Rico San Diego will give the San Diego community the opportunity to be exposed to the Puerto Rican culture, history, traditions and contributions made by Puerto Ricans to the United States of America.

The House of Puerto Rico San Diego is seeking funding to cover the construction of a one thousand square foot (1,000 sq. ft.) custom built cottage in Balboa Park, San Diego, California. The cottage will be an addition to the already existing nineteen cottages that represent the House of Pacific Relations International Cottages, Inc.
