Greetings!

We are the House of Puerto Rico San Diego organization. The city of San Diego has invited us to join other nations by building a community house at the world-famous Balboa Park. This is quite an honor for our organization and it represents a golden opportunity towards addressing our objectives:

    * Promote a better understanding amongst people of all nationalities and races,
    * Promote and cultivate the Puerto Rican culture and
    * Promote social and educational activities amongst its members.

Being that Balboa Park is a primary location for visitors to San Diego, the HPR-SD community cottage will allow us to expand our objectives worldwide.

To build this structure fundraising is requires. Through HPR-SD membership activities and donations, we have $33,000 to go. Obviously, we need corporate help if we are to reach our goal and maintain the enthusiasm of our membership.
