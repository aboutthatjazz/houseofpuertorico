House of Puerto Rico in the News!

Annie Zendejas, one of the recipients of the 2001 HPRSD Scholarship, delighted members of HPRSD with the history of Salsa and provided lessons. The occasion was highlighted in a recent article Dana Littlefield in the San Diego Union Tribune. Annie's contibution to the Culture Corner is a part of the monthly HPRSD membership meeting. The article also highlights the HPRSD's efforts to raise funds for the construction of the Casita in Balboa Park.
