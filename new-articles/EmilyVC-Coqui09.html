Cute As A Coqui
by Emily Velez-Confer

Return to Reinitas

Art Class - March 28, 2009

Many of you may know the saying Cute as a Button, but around our Casita we should say As Cute as a Coqui.

Why you might ask?

Because on Saturday, March 28, 2009, 7 members and guest came to make Coquis out of a plastic bottle, construction paper and some paint. And they turned out As Cute as a Coqui!

Three Coquis by the Hector/Martinez girls.
Photo by Emily VC.

The Hector/Martinez girls, Irene, Hiram, Judy and Clarissa (a military officer, visiting from out of town) braved the art class project with excitement, determination and creativity! 

Three lovely young ladies, one young man and three ladies young at heart show off their Coquis – ENJOY!
