House of Puerto Rico 2009
Annual Día de San Juan Picnic
June 20, 2009
by Emmy Martinez

It was an overcast day, a bit cool too.  Yet people strolled in to Admiral Baker Park as singles or in groups.  There were big people and little people—people of all shape, size, and colors.  They were all here for the House of Puerto Rico's Annual Día de San Juan Picnic.  In all, it was estimated that over 150 people attended. 

Music was presented by DJ Rick (La Voz) Chriss; there was laughter, there were contests, and there was food—lots of food.  Everyone brought his or her own food and drinks, some even grilled their steaks on the grill provided by the park.

Faces were painted with the Puerto Rican flag by Emily Velez-Confer and helpers.  Jasmine Flores presided over the games for the children and Angie Freeman coordinated the scavenger hunt. Both were a blast.

Gene Perry presented the trophy (dedicated to his late father, Tomás Perry) to the Domino1st place winners of the 3rd Annual Domino Tournament, Benjamin Cruz and Don Pedro.  Bobby Rivera and Jesse Rivera came in second place.

In the dance category, Casilda Pagán and Sonny Thompson came in 1st place.  In second place the winners were Alex Swaisgood and Eva Flores (teen category).  The Reinitas and the Jovenes/Vejigantes danced a typical Puerto Rico "Danza" and "Plena" dances and looked great in their costumes.

It was a great day and a good time was had by all.
