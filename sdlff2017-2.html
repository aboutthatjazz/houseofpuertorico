Pepo Pa'l Senado
Spotlight Films Showcase

Dir.  Raúl García
(2016, 122 mins, Comedy, Puerto Rico, Spanish with English subtitles)

In this political satire, a ruthless political adviser sets out to prove her claim that she can get anyone elected to the Senate by setting her sights on Pepo González, an ordinary, unemployed citizen. (SDLFF)

Fate brings Pepo González (René Monclova), an ordinary, unemployed citizen, before an unscrupulous former political adviser (Marian Pabon). Her plan: to select a total stranger, without qualities or political lineage, and take the Capitol during one of the most important elections in the history of Puerto Rico. Will she be able to get Pepo a seat in the Senate? (IMDB)

R rated.



Yo Soy Político
Spotlight Films Showcase

Dir. Javier Colón Ríos
(2016, 90 mins, Comedy, Puerto Rico, Spanish)

Carlos is an ex-convict looking for a job where he can make a lot of money without putting in a lot of work. After discussing it with his cellmate; Cangri; they decide that the only job that fits that description is governor of Puerto Rico.

Rated - not available at this time
